Fruity Oatmeal

A homemade, tasteful, fruity oatmeal breakfast, to supply you with the necessary nutrients for a productive day!

Ingredients

  • 50g oatmeal.
  • 200ml milk.
  • 100g raspberries.
  • 1 banana.
  • 1 scoop protein powder.

Instructions

  1. Add 50g oatmeal into a bowl.
  2. Pour 200ml milk into to bowl.
  3. Microwave for 2-3 mins.
  4. After it's done, take the bowl out of the microwave and add 1scoop of protein powder, mixing it with the oatmeal.
  5. Add the sliced banana into the bowl, followed by the rapsberries.
  6. Enjoy your nutritious breakfast!
